About me

I am a visiting PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University since September 2022, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Elisa Bertino. I am a forth-year PhD student at School of Cyber Engineering, Xidian Univeristy, under the supervision of Professor Xiaofeng Chen. Previously, I obtained my M.S degree from Communication University of China in 2019 and B.S. degree from Huaiyin Institute of Technology in 2017.

I am interested in trusted AI research. My recent research focuses on evaluating and improving the robustness of deep learning models in image classification and intrusion detection tasks, with particular interests in adversarial testing (e.g. adversarial example attack) and robustness verification (e.g. robustness lower bound estimation). I also had some research experience in secure video encoding, such as AVC and HEVC.
