About me

I am a visiting PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University since September 2022, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Elisa Bertino, the head of Cyber Space Security Lab. I am a fifth-year PhD student in the School of Cyber Engineering at Xidian Univeristy under the supervision of Professor Xiaofeng Chen, the head of Ruiyun Data Security Lab. Previously, I obtained my M.S degree from Communication University of China in 2019 and B.S. degree from Huaiyin Institute of Technology in 2017.

I am interested in trustworthy Artificial Intelligence. My recent research focuses on robustness issues in deep learning models with a particular interest in adversarial attacks and robustness certification.
